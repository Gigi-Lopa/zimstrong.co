OUR SERVICES

Pillars

We also make 2, 1m: 2.2m and 2.3m long pillars with the thickness of 25mm and 30mm. During the production our pillars we also use deformed bars to ensure that there quality is guaranteed

Pavers

We have different designs of pavers which include (Holland, Interlocking and hexagon, 3D Arrows, 3D diamond and 3D). Our pavers are machine compressed made from mixing PPC cement and Quarry dust making them very strong.

Roofing Tiles

Zimstrong investments is also involved in the production of double romain concrete roofing tiles with different colours such as green, blue black and maroon. Our tiles are very strong and eye catching.
