ABOUT US

Background


Zimstrong Investment Company is a Harare based brick making company that was established in 2019 to fill a gap in the sector of manufacturing and selling building materials to the local people and all over the country.

Therefore one of our objectives as a company is to expand our product line by investing in quality cement bricks, pavers and roofing tiles.

The Management has prospered in placing the group ahead of the competition by following to strict policy of superior quality and customer service. To maintain good relationships with our customers, suppliers, contractors, partners and other key stakeholders we ensure high levels of professionalism, integrity and honesty.

Our Mission


We aim to keep on producing better products and services that will enhance the standards of living and the well-being of our society. This will be achieved through the commitment and dedication of our team and the support of our suppliers. Providing our customers with exceptional service, quality, and value will brace our position and deliver superb returns to our partners and shareholders.
